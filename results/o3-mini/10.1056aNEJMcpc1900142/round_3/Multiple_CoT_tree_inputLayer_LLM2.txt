**3. Differential Diagnosis Analysis:**

* **Possible Diagnostic Directions:**  Metabolic acidosis, gastrointestinal bleeding, substance intoxication/withdrawal, infection, cardiac arrhythmia.
* **Potential Diagnoses:**
